JavaScript Basic - Tip calculator

Dasun and his friends went on a picnic. To get food they went to 3 different resturants for each meal. The bills are Rs.1230, Rs.982, Rs.1640.

To tip the waiter a fair amount, Dasun created a simple tip calculator (as a function). He likes to tip
20% of the bill when the bill is less than Rs.1000,
15% when the bill is between Rs 1000 and Rs 1500, and the
10% if the bill is more than Rs 1500.

In the end, Dasun would like to have 2 arrays:

  1. Containing all 3 tips
  2. Containing all three final paid amount(bill+tip)
